Meet Krystal Ann

Photography rooted in real life

Hey there — I'm Krystal, an Omaha-based photographer who found her way back to photography through the sidelines of a softball field and the living rooms of the families I love.

I specialize in family portraits, sports action, couples and engagement, and children's milestone sessions. My approach is simple: I want you to feel at ease, laugh a little, and forget a camera is pointed your way — because that's when the best photos happen.

Whether we're in a golden field at sunset or your own backyard, I'm here to create images that feel like you.

The Process

Booking is simple & stress-free

1

Reach Out

Fill out the contact form or send me a message. Tell me what you're envisioning — no detail is too small.

2

We Chat

I'll reach back out to talk through your vision, pick a date, and find the perfect spot in Omaha or beyond.

3

Your Session

Show up as you are — I handle the rest. We'll laugh, explore, and make images you'll actually love.

4

Get Your Gallery

Your edited photos arrive in an online gallery within 2–3 weeks — ready to download, print, and share.
