Session Type 01

Omaha Family Photography

Your family is one of a kind. These sessions are designed to capture the way you actually are — together, a little chaotic, and completely real.

Family sessions are relaxed and unhurried. We find a beautiful outdoor location in or around Omaha — a park, a field, your neighborhood — and just hang out. I'll give gentle prompts to get you moving and interacting, but I'm mostly documenting what's already there.

Kids run, parents laugh, and grandparents beam. That's the good stuff, and I don't miss it.

This session is for you if…

  • Your kids are "not good at photos" — neither are most kids, honestly
  • You want photos that look like your family, not a stock catalog
  • You keep putting it off because life is busy — let's fix that

Session Type 02

Omaha Sports Photography

Sports photography is where my passion was reignited — on the sidelines of a youth softball field, camera in hand, heart full.

I photograph athletes in action and in portrait — capturing not just the sport, but the personality, the grit, and the joy behind it. Whether it's a game-day shoot, a senior athlete portrait, or action shots at practice, I bring the same energy you bring to the field.

These sessions work great as standalone athlete portraits or combined with a senior session to show off a sport that's defined your high school years.

This session is for you if…

  • You have a senior athlete whose sport has defined their high school years
  • You want action shots that capture the grit and joy, not just posed headshots
  • Your athlete deserves photos as serious as their dedication

Session Type 03

Omaha Couples & Engagement Photography

Love looks best when it's unscripted. Couples sessions are meant to feel like a date — somewhere you both love, doing what comes naturally.

Engagement sessions are great for save-the-dates, social media announcements, and — honestly — just having beautiful photos of the two of you before life gets busy with wedding planning.

But you don't have to be engaged. Anniversaries, "just because," relationship milestones — all welcome. I photograph love at every stage.

I'll help you pick a location that means something to you, give you a little direction to get started, and then let the natural moments take over.

This session is for you if…

  • You just got engaged and want save-the-date photos that actually feel like you
  • You want photos of the two of you before life gets busier (it always does)
  • You're not a "pose and smile" couple — you want something candid and real

Session Type 04

Omaha Children & Milestone Photography

They grow so fast it's almost not fair. Children's sessions capture who they are right now — at this exact, fleeting, never-again age.

I photograph everything from newborns to teenagers. Whether it's a first birthday, a "turning 5" session, or a simple portrait to commemorate childhood, these sessions are paced for kids — not the other way around.

I'm patient. I'm silly. I've been chased by enough toddlers to know that the best photos often happen when no one is looking at the camera.

Mini sessions (shorter, lower-investment) are also available seasonally for newborns, 6-month sitters, cake smashes, and holiday portraits. Ask about upcoming mini session dates.

This session is for you if…

  • You're terrified of how fast they're growing and want to press pause
  • You have a milestone birthday, newborn, or "just because" moment to celebrate
  • You need a photographer who actually has patience for toddlers
