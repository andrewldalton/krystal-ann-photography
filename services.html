Session Type 01

Family

Your family is one of a kind. These sessions are designed to capture the way you actually are — together, a little chaotic, and completely real.

Family sessions are relaxed and unhurried. We find a beautiful outdoor location in or around Omaha — a park, a field, your neighborhood — and just hang out. I'll give gentle prompts to get you moving and interacting, but I'm mostly documenting what's already there.

Children & Milestone Photos

Ask Me Anything

What should we wear?

Coordinate without matching. Think tones and textures — creams, earth tones, soft blues and greens. Avoid big logos or bright neons. When in doubt, I'll send you a style guide before your session.

What if my kids won't cooperate?

They never do at first — and that's totally fine. I build in wiggle time, I bring patience, and I've found that the "chaos" moments often make the best photos. Don't stress.

How far in advance should I book?

As soon as possible, especially for fall sessions (September–October books out fast). Spring and summer also fill quickly. I recommend reaching out 4–8 weeks before your ideal date.

What if the weather is bad?

We'll reschedule. Nebraska weather is unpredictable and I never want to rush a session because of clouds or cold. We'll find another date that works.

Do you travel outside Omaha?

Yes — I regularly photograph in Papillion, Bellevue, La Vista, and surrounding areas. For destinations further out, travel fees may apply. Just ask.
